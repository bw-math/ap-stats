Point Estimation#

A sample of data is characterized by point estimates of sample statistics.

Definitions#

Observation

A single piece of data. The subscript is called the index of the observation. If the sample is ordered, the index corresponds to the order in which the observation was made, i.e. is the first observation, is the second observation, etc.

Sample

A collection, or set, of observations. The number of samples, n, is called the sample size.

Frequency

The number of times a particular observation occurs in a sample of data.
